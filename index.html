OXIDATION STATE:-

The apparent charge on an atom of an element in a molecule
or in an ion is called oxidation number or oxidation state.

EXAMPLE:

1:- Oxidation Number of Cr is +6 in K 2Cr 2O 7

2:-Oxidation Number of CL is +5 in Ca(ClO 3) 2
